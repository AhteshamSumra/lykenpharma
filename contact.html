FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products available without a prescription? +
Some of our products like Protiken (nutritional supplement) may be available over the counter. However, pharmaceutical products like Gabapent-B, Livoken-M, and Carticor-UC should be used under physician guidance. Always consult your doctor before starting any new medication.
How can healthcare professionals partner with Lyken Pharma? +
We welcome partnerships with doctors, hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. Contact us through this form or call our medical representative team to discuss how we can work together to serve your patients better.
Do you provide product samples for clinical evaluation? +
Yes, we provide product samples to registered medical practitioners for clinical evaluation. Please contact us with your medical registration details and we'll arrange sample delivery to your clinic or hospital.
What is the typical delivery time for bulk orders? +
Standard bulk orders are typically processed within 3–5 business days and delivered within 7–10 business days across India. Express delivery options are available for urgent requirements. Contact our sales team for specific delivery timelines.
Are there any contraindications I should know about? +
Each product has specific contraindications and precautions that are detailed in the product insert. We recommend consulting the full prescribing information and consulting with a qualified healthcare professional before recommending our products to patients.
