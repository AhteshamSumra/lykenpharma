Who We Are

Pharmaceutical Innovation with Heart

Lyken Pharma was founded on a simple yet powerful belief — that every patient deserves access to scientifically formulated, high-quality medicines that genuinely work. Our journey began over 15 years ago with a small team of passionate pharmacists and physicians determined to make a difference.

Today, we offer a comprehensive range of pharmaceutical products spanning bone health, joint care, liver protection, nutrition, and pain management. Each formulation is the result of extensive research, clinical validation, and pharmaceutical-grade manufacturing.

Based in Rajkot, Gujarat, we serve healthcare professionals and patients across India, building long-lasting relationships rooted in trust, transparency, and therapeutic excellence.
